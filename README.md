### Project Description
The daily affirmation apps helps women improve mental well-being by providing one positive message each day to encourage confidence ,positive and emotional balance

### The Problem statement
Many women experience daily stress and self doubt ,but access to mental health support is limitted and emotional well being is often overlooked

### The Solution
Helping users built a habit of self care and emotional awarness in a simple ,non clinical way
